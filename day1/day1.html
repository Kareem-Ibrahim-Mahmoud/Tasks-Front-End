Mr. Bean is a popular British comedic character created and portrayed by actor Rowan Atkinson. The character first appeared on television in a show titled Mr. Bean, which aired from 1990 to 1995, gaining widespread popularity for its unique style of physical and visual humor. Mr. Bean has since become a global icon, with additional movies, an animated series, and numerous adaptations. Mr. Bean is characterized as a childlike adult who often finds himself in awkward, humorous situations due to his clumsy, curious, and often quirky behavior. The character rarely speaks, relying instead on exaggerated facial expressions, physical comedy, and limited vocal sounds. His classic car is a light green 1977 British Leyland Mini, which he drives in unique, sometimes bizarre ways.
